How to Use the Extension

  1. Fill out your profile information on the left
  2. Navigate to a job application form
  3. Click the extension icon or use Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+Y shortcut
  4. The extension will detect and map form fields to your profile
  5. Review the autofill preview and confirm to fill the form
  6. Submit the form manually after verification

Field Mappings

First Name

Mapped from: #jobFirstName → Profile.firstName

Last Name

Mapped from: #jobLastName → Profile.lastName

Email

Mapped from: #jobEmail → Profile.email

Phone

Mapped from: #jobPhone → Profile.phone
